When should I expect my order to arrive?

  • Orders usually take:
    • Cairo and Alexandria: from 24 to 48 hours
    • Delta and Canal Cities: within 72 hours
    • Upper Egypt and Hurghada: from 4 to 5 days
  • Business days are Sunday-Thursday, excluding bank holidays and weekends.
  • For orders placed after 1 PM, the delivery time will be calculated from 8 AM the following morning.
  • We'll send you a tracking number within 24 hours of you placing your order.

Please note that every seller has a different cutoff time for orders that ship the day they are placed. It's not a guarantee that an order will ship the same day it's placed.

During the holiday season, the influx of orders and severe weather conditions can delay the delivery of your order. For larger items, delivery times may be longer.
